## 🎓 Learning Path

### Junior → Mid-Level (Modules 1-4)
Complete beginner challenges to understand Go syntax and basic data structures.

### Mid-Level → Senior (Modules 5-9)
Master interfaces, error handling, and standard library usage.

### Senior → Expert (Modules 10-13)
Deep dive into concurrency, performance, and production patterns.
